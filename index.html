Study Guide – Exam Domains

1. Author & Maintain Workflows (40%)

  • Triggers: push, pull_request, schedule, workflow_dispatch, repository_dispatch
  • YAML syntax, jobs → steps, matrix, needs, if:
  • Secrets, GITHUB_TOKEN permissions, environment variables
  • Artifacts, caching, reusable workflows
  • Service containers, CodeQL, releases

2. Consume Workflows (20%)

  • View logs, debug logging, status badges
  • Environments, required reviewers, branch protection
  • Download/upload artifacts

3. Author & Maintain Actions (25%)

  • JavaScript vs Docker vs Composite actions
  • action.yml inputs/outputs
  • Workflow commands (::set-output, ::add-mask, etc.)

4. Enterprise Management (15%)

  • Self-hosted runners & runner groups
  • Organization secrets & policies
  • Reusable workflows & starter workflows
